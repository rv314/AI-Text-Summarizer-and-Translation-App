AI Text Summarizer and Translation App

Welcome to the AI Text Summarizer App! This app leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence APIs to provide concise summaries of long texts. Whether you have a lengthy article, research paper, or any other text document that you want to summarize quickly, our app can assist you. If required the summarized text can also be translated to Chinese language (currently only available in one language)

1. Simply paste your text into the text area below and click the "Summarize" button.
2. Select checkbox 'Translate to Chinese' if required.
